# AI Job Market Analysis – Project Overview

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze trends in the **Artificial Intelligence job market** by examining factors such as salary distribution, job demand, education requirements, geographical salary differences, and experience levels. The goal is to generate insights that help understand **which roles, skills, and locations are most valuable in the AI industry**.

---

# Dataset Overview

The dataset contains **1500 job records** related to AI and machine learning roles. It includes information such as:

* Job Title
* Industry
* Country / City
* Salary (USD)
* Education Requirement
* Experience Level
* Job Type (Remote / Hybrid / On-site)
* Demand Score

These variables allow analysis of **salary trends, demand patterns, and workforce distribution in AI jobs**.

---

# Key Insights from the Dashboard

## 1. Salary Distribution by Country

The analysis shows strong geographic differences in AI salaries.

* **USA** offers the highest average salary (~$230K).
* **India** shows the lowest average salary (~$130K).
* Other high-paying countries include **UAE, Switzerland, and Australia**.

This highlights the **impact of geographic location on compensation in AI careers**.

---

## 2. Most In-Demand AI Roles

Demand scores indicate which roles are most sought after.

Top demanded roles include:

* **LLM Engineer**
* **ML Engineer**
* **AI Agent Developer**
* **Senior ML Engineer**
* **Generative AI Engineer**

These roles are driving the current **AI technology boom**.

The **least demanded roles** include:

* AI Compliance Manager
* AI Ethics Officer
* AI Security Engineer

These are emerging governance roles that may grow in the future.

---

## 3. Education vs Salary

Education level influences salary outcomes.

Highest average salaries:

* **PhD (~$210K)**
* **Master’s (~$210K)**

Lower salary levels:

* **Associate degree (~$180K)**

This indicates advanced education can significantly increase **earning potential in AI careers**.

---

## 4. Job Type Salary Comparison

Comparison of work modes shows:

* **Fully Remote** jobs offer slightly higher salaries.
* **On-site and Hybrid roles** have similar average compensation.

This suggests remote work is becoming competitive in the AI job market.

---

## 5. Highest Paying Cities

Top paying cities include:

* **New York**
* **San Francisco**
* **Seattle**
* **Chicago**

These are major **technology hubs with strong AI demand**.

---

## 6. Experience Level Distribution

Job counts by experience level show a balanced market:

* Entry Level – 385 jobs
* Lead Level – 381 jobs
* Mid Level – 370 jobs
* Senior Level – 364 jobs

This suggests opportunities exist **across all career stages** in AI.
